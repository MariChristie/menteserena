About Me and the Therapeutic Journey

I am Ana Silva, a clinical psychologist CRP XX/XXXXX, specializing in general psychology. My work is focused on helping people transform challenges into opportunities for growth. In an environment of total confidentiality, I use an integrative approach to build, in partnership with you, the necessary tools to deal with anxiety, improve relationships, and strengthen your emotional resilience.

I believe that therapy is an investment in yourself. If you are seeking more clarity and well-being for your life, I am here to help.
